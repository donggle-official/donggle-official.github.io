Our Story
우리 이야기

Built by Fans,
for Fans.

팬이 만든,
팬을 위한 서비스.

Why "Donggle"? Our name has a double meaning that reflects who we are.

First, it's inspired by our beloved dog — a little pup who represents the care and warmth we put into every package. You'll see paw prints in our logo to remind you that your items are handled with that same love.

Second, it comes from the Korean word "Dong-geul-da (둥글다)", which means "to be round." Just like the Earth, we believe that K-goods should be able to reach any corner of the globe. Our mission is to bridge the distance between Korea and fans worldwide, ensuring your treasures travel safely and come full circle right to your door.

We started Donggle because we were fans too. We know the frustration of missing a limited release or struggling with complex checkout systems. That's why we created a service that treats every order with the same care we'd want for our own collections.

동글은 저희 반려견 이름에서 유래했어요. 🐶

발바닥 로고는 동글을 믿고 찾아와주신 모든 고객님들이 남긴 발자취를 의미합니다.

2021년 트위터에서 구매/결제대행으로 시작해 고객분들의 요청으로 배송대행까지 확장하게 되었습니다. 저희도 팬이기 때문에, 한정판을 놓치는 아쉬움과 해외 결제의 어려움을 잘 알아요. 그래서 모든 주문을 내 컬렉션처럼 소중히 다루는 서비스를 만들었습니다.
