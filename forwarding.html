📋 Forwarding Request — Terms & Conditions

Please read the following carefully. You must agree to proceed with your forwarding request.

1. Receiving Fee

A receiving fee of ₩3,000 per package applies to all incoming shipments. This includes basic exterior photo documentation and free consolidation.

2. Storage Policy

Free storage is provided for up to 30 days from the date of arrival. After 30 days, an extended storage fee of ₩500 per day per package will be charged. Packages stored beyond 90 days may be disposed of at Donggle's discretion after prior notice.

3. Tracking Number Registration

You must register your tracking number before your package arrives. Packages arriving without a pre-registered tracking number may experience delays in processing. Donggle is not responsible for delays caused by missing tracking information.

4. Package Inspection

Donggle performs a basic exterior inspection upon arrival. We do not open packages unless a customizing service has been requested. Any damage caused during domestic delivery (before arrival at our warehouse) is not our responsibility.

5. Prohibited Items

The following items are strictly prohibited: lithium batteries (standalone), flammable materials, pressurized cans, explosives/weapons, alcohol/tobacco/e-cigarettes, pharmaceuticals/vitamins, perishable food, counterfeit goods, cash/lottery tickets, adult content, ivory products. For perfumes, candles, electronics, and cosmetics — please contact us before sending.

6. Box Surcharge

Box surcharges are based on the final outgoing package dimensions, not the incoming box size. Oversized or overweight packages will be notified before processing.

7. Customizing Services

Unboxing, inclusion-only shipping, and battery removal services require your consent. All customizing services are video-recorded for dispute prevention. Disposal of items requires explicit written consent.

8. Fixed Pricing

All fees are fixed and non-negotiable. Individual discounts are not available. Payment must be made within the specified deadline; orders are automatically cancelled if payment is not received.

9. Shipping Fees

Shipping fees are billed separately based on actual weight and destination. EMS official rates apply. Donggle is not responsible for customs duties, taxes, or import fees in the destination country.

10. US Customers — Important Notice

As of August 29, 2025, the US de minimis exemption (previously $800) has been eliminated. All packages shipped to the US are subject to a 15% customs duty (IEEPA basis). These charges are the sole responsibility of the recipient (customer).

11. Dispute Resolution

Packaging videos are used as evidence in the event of a dispute. Donggle is not responsible for damage or loss caused by the shipping carrier after the package has been handed over.

12. Video Recording Policy

The entire process from receipt to packaging and dispatch is video-recorded using a top-view smartphone setup. Videos are stored in Google Photos and may be provided as evidence in case of disputes.
